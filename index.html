Explore Caracas

Discover rich history and breathtaking mountain views in Venezuela's vibrant mountain-valley capital.

Top three activities to do at Caracas

Visit the Simón Bolívar Birthplace Museum

Step into the home of El Libertador. A historic colonial gem in the heart of Caracas.

Check out El Ávila National Park

Ascend for breathtaking mountain views and nature trails high above the city.

Take a walk along the Los Próceres Promenade

Stroll along this grand historic landmark dedicated to Venezuela's national heroes.
